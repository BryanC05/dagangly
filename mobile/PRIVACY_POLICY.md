# Privacy Policy for MSME Marketplace

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

**Contact:** support@msmemarketplace.com

## Introduction

MSME Marketplace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register
- **Business Information:** Business name, address, and registration details for sellers
- **Profile Information:** Profile pictures and business logos you upload
- **Location Data:** Precise location data to show nearby sellers and products

### Usage Data
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
- **App Usage:** Pages visited, features used, and time spent in the app
- **Transaction Data:** Order history, payment information (processed securely via third-party providers)

### Communication Data
- **Chat Messages:** Messages exchanged between buyers and sellers
- **Support Requests:** Communications with our customer support team

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Provide Services:** Facilitate buying, selling, and delivery services
2. **Location Services:** Show nearby sellers and enable delivery tracking
3. **Communication:** Enable chat between buyers and sellers
4. **Notifications:** Send order updates, promotional offers, and account alerts
5. **Improvement:** Analyze usage to improve app features and user experience
6. **Security:** Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

## Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Other Users:** Your business profile is visible to other marketplace users
- **Service Providers:** Delivery partners, payment processors, and cloud hosting providers
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored securely on cloud servers with encryption
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Payment information is processed by PCI-compliant third parties

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Disable location services through device settings

## Location Data

Our app collects location data to:
- Show nearby sellers and products
- Enable delivery tracking
- Calculate shipping distances

You can disable location access in your device settings, but some features may not function properly.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Maps:** For location services and mapping
- **Firebase:** For push notifications and analytics
- **Payment Gateways:** For secure payment processing

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
